**STARTUP PLAN: Launch a fitness app!**  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  

**1. MISSION**  
Empower busy urban professionals to build lifelong fitness habits with AI‑driven, calendar‑aware 5‑15 minute micro‑workouts that fit into any free slot and are reinforced by social gamification.  

**2. THE PROBLEM**  
Professionals know they should exercise but lack a single, personalized tool that (a) automatically finds tiny time windows, (b) delivers instant workouts, and (c) keeps them accountable. Existing apps require manual planning and provide weak social incentives, causing >70 % dropout within three months.  

**3. TARGET CUSTOMER**  
- Age 25‑38, $70‑120 k yr income, urban + remote professionals.  
- Own a smartwatch/phone, spend > 5 h/week on screens, have tried generic fitness apps or gyms and quit within 3 months.  
- Goal‑oriented, love data, enjoy short “micro‑workouts”, respond to peer challenges and small rewards.  

**4. BUSINESS MODEL**  
| Tier | Price | What’s Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | Calendar sync, 2 micro‑workouts/week, basic badge system. |
| Pro (B2C) | $12 /mo or $120 /yr (2 wk free) | Unlimited AI‑personalized micro‑workouts, full badge/reward access, ad‑free. |
| Enterprise | $9 /emp /mo (min 100 emp) | Admin dashboard, corporate challenges, branded analytics, bulk billing. |

**5. FINANCIAL SNAPSHOT**  
- **Monthly burn:** ≈ $106,800 (≈ $1.28 M / yr) – lean core team, cloud, marketing.  
- **Funding needed to ship MVP (90 d):** $428 k (covers 3 mo salaries, recruitment, infra, legal, beta incentives). Recommended round ≈ $500 k to add a 2‑month buffer.  
- **Runway with $500 k:** ~ 4.7 months at full burn; can extend to ~ 6 months by trimming headcount & ad spend (≈ $85 k /mo).  
- **Break‑even:** ~ 9‑10 k paying users (or 4‑5 corporate pilots of ~250 emp) → $1.2‑$1.4 M ARR ≈ $106 k /mo revenue.  

**6. GO‑TO‑MARKET (FIRST 30 DAYS)**  
1. **Paid short‑form video (IG Reels/TikTok):** 3 creative 5‑sec “5‑min break? Start now” clips; $4.5 k test budget, aim CPI ≤ $0.90 (CAC ≤ $12).  
2. **LinkedIn B2B outreach:** 150 targeted HR/People‑Ops inboxes + $2 k InMail spend; offer a free 30‑day “data‑driven wellness challenge” to secure ≥ 2 pilots.  
3. **Discord community launch + referral flow:** Invite 150 beta users, run a daily “gap‑post” challenge; embed unique referral codes that grant 1 wk free for both parties.  

**7. PRODUCT MVP (5 FEATURES)**  
1. Rule‑based calendar‑aware gap‑filler engine (detects 5‑15 min free slots).  
2. Library of 20 pre‑designed body‑weight micro‑workouts (JSON recipes).  
3. Real‑time push notification with “Start Now” deep link.  
4. Digital badge & streak gamification (no external rewards).  
5. Referral/invite‑a‑friend flow granting a free week to both parties.  

**8. REVENUE TARGETS**  
- **Day 30:** $3.5 k MRR (≈ 250 paying B2C users).  
- **Day 60:** $15 k MRR (≈ 600 B2C + 2 enterprise pilots ≈ $1.8 k MRR).  
- **Day 90:** $43 k MRR (≈ 1 050 B2C + 4 pilots ≈ $4.5 k MRR).  

**9. KEY CONFLICTS DETECTED**  
1. **CAC Assumptions:** Finance warned the $15 k ad budget understates true CPI; marketing proposed a $2 k test first.  
2. **AI‑engine Timeline:** Product demanded a full ML fatigue model in 8 weeks; finance and product insisted on a rule‑based engine first.  
3. **Rewards Marketplace Timing:** CEO wanted the external points‑to‑rewards store live at launch; finance and product flagged legal, partner, and cost risks and recommended delaying until ≥ 5 k DAU.  

**10. FINAL VERDICT**  
The concept is viable – the market need (time‑scarcity + habit loop) is clear and the lean MVP can be built in 8 weeks for ≤ $500 k. Success hinges on keeping CAC ≤ $30, securing early enterprise pilots with cash‑in‑advance, and avoiding scope creep (no ML or external marketplace until proven traction). If acquisition costs explode, the AI engine stalls, or the rewards partner never materialises, the runway evaporates and the startup will run out of cash before hitting break‑even.
